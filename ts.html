ABOUT THE CAFE

Daisy's Cafe is a specialty coffee shop that focuses on coffee, food, community and culture. In a spirit of constant change, we actively integrate food and art into our daily lives.

Whether you're here for coffee, a light lunch, or to unwind after a busy day, we hope you'll find fun and a connection to someone or something - and everything wonderful in Between.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet.

Chef, Coffeeist and Owner: Daisy Zhuang

Opening hours: everyday from 8am to 9pm.

Address: Tai Kwun, 10 Hollywood Road, Central, Hong Kong SAR, China
